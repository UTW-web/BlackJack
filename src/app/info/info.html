EDGE 21

I built EDGE 21 because I was tired of people playing Blackjack on "luck." I wanted the truth—I wanted to know exactly why the cards fall the way they do. What started as my own personal project to translate complex math into usable code has grown into a toolkit I use every day to gain an edge over the casino. I’m sharing this with you so you can stop guessing and start playing with real confidence. Whether you’re using the EV Calculator to spot an advantage, mastering S17/H17 deviations, or sharpening your skills in the Simulation Engine, this is my personal training ground—and now, it’s yours. Let’s stop gambling and start playing the math.

EV Calculator

I built this calculator because "guessing" isn't a strategy—it's a recipe for going broke. This tool is my way of stripping away the mystery behind a gambling session. By inputting your bankroll, table rules, and how you bet at different counts, you get a clear picture of your actual hourly profit (EV) and, more importantly, your Risk of Ruin. It’s the cold, hard math that tells you whether you're actually beating the house or just playing with fire. Use this to protect your bankroll and see if your current strategy is sustainable in the long run.

Charts & Deviations

Knowing the Basic Strategy is your foundation, but the deviations are where you actually start to turn a profit. I’ve integrated these reference charts from Blackjack Apprenticeship into the app because I believe they provide the most accurate roadmap for any serious player. Whether you're playing standard games, ENHC, or adjusting for S17/H17 rules, these are the moments where you move beyond standard strategy and start playing the count. My goal is to give you a clear, easy-to-use guide so you don't have to overthink—just study, execute, and let the math handle the rest.
